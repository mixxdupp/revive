Trauma Programs

ACS trauma programs advance trauma care nationwide through education, quality improvement, accreditation, system development, advocacy, and injury prevention.

ACS Hires Medical Director, Trauma Education

Jeffrey D. Kerby, MD, PhD, FACS, has been appointed ACS Medical Director, Trauma Education, effective March 16. A globally recognized trauma surgeon, researcher, and US Air Force veteran, Dr. Kerby will provide strategic, clinical, and scientific leadership for key ACS trauma education initiatives in the US and internationally.
