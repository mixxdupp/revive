Course Information Training Providers

Rescue 3 International is an international accrediting body for state-of-the-art technical rescue courses. We develop training courses used by organizations and individuals operating in high-risk environments.

Since 1979, Rescue 3 has set the global standard for technical rescue training, offering accreditation, guidance and support for maintaining best practice. We offer a full accreditation framework for the people, training providers and organizations that deliver and use these courses.
