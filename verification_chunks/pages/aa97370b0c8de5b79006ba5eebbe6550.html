The IAEM Network Is One of Our Best Assets - Learn How IAEM Benefits Its Members



 

Access

IAEM members have access to the largest network of top emergency management experts who can offer solutions, guidance and assistance.

Information

Members receive regular updates on issues of importance via email, social media, weekly e-newsletter (the IAEM Dispatch), and monthly newsletter, (the IAEM Bulletin)

Professional Development

Opportunities to learn or to share knowledge are provided via in-person meetings, networking, continuing education and training opportunities.

Jobs Board

Members may learn about professional job opportunities in emergency management and seek individuals for positions needed by their departments. [View jobs]

Representation

IAEM participates on government working groups addressing vital issues such as terrorism preparedness, emergency management program standards, communications, disaster assistance delivery and others.

IAEM in Action! 

IAEM Testimony on Palisades Fire One Year Later

January 28, 2026 – IAEM-USA Government Affairs Chair Spencer Hawkins, CEM, testified before the Senate Permanent Subcommittee on Investigations on One Year After Disaster: Experts Weigh in on the Palisades Fire. 

Networking in Louisville

Nov. 2025 – A group of attendees captured a moment together between sessions during another busy conference day at the 73rd IAEM Annual Conference & EMEX. From L-R: Alberto Martinez, CEM; Richard Diaz; IAEM Latin America President Gustavo Reina, CEM; Yesenia Arreguin, CEM; Elizabeth Russell; and IAEM USA First Vice President Josh Morton, CEM.

A Puppy Break in EMEX

Nov. 2025 – During the EMEX Opening Reception, Michael Teener, CEM, and Roni Fraser joined fellow attendees in petting the puppies and enjoying a lighthearted break at the WAGS booth. Thank you to WAGS for joining us this year! 

Honoring Beth Armstrong

Nov. 2025 – At the 73rd IAEM Annual Conference, Beth Armstrong, CAE, MAM, was recognized during the Monday morning opening session for her 40 years of service and leadership with IAEM. 

New Certification Commissioner Training

Nov. 2025 – New members of the IAEM Certification Commission spent Nov. 16 in Louisville training new commissioners. IAEM thanks these volunteers for their time and expertise in helping emergency managers earn their AEM and CEM credentials.

EMEX Ribbon Cutting Excitement

Nov. 2025 – Every year at the IAEM Annual Conference, we celebrate the opening of the EMEX Hall with a ribbon cutting. This year, IAEM welcomed 150 exhibitors to Louisville. Thank you to the incredible companies and organizations from every sector of the emergency management industry for sharing your knowledge, skills and products.

Region 4 in North Carolina

Oct. 27–30 – IAEM-USA Region 4 was proud to represent IAEM at the North Carolina Emergency Management Association Conference in Wilmington, NC. With more than 300 attendees, the team shared information on IAEM membership, certification, and the upcoming Annual Conference.

L-R: Drew Pearson, CEM; Dr. Christa Lopez, Ph.D., CEM; Wike Graham, CEM; and Will Ray, Director of North Carolina Emergency Management.

NEMA Annual Forum

October 9-12, 2025 – IAEM-USA President Carrie Speranza, CEM, represented IAEM at the legislative committee session during the NEMA Annual Forum in Cheyenne, Wyoming. She emphasized that our nation’s strength depends on elevating local perspectives and fostering an unbreakable bond between state and local emergency managers.

IAEM Leaders Prepare for the Year Ahead

Incoming IAEM Executive Director Nicole Blankenship, CAE, met with incoming IAEM-USA President Josh Morton, CEM, in Saluda County, SC, from September 30-October 1 to discuss priorities and goals for the year ahead.

Tour of the SC State EOC

Incoming IAEM Executive Director Nicole Blankenship, CAE, received a tour of the South Carolina State Emergency Operations Center while meeting with incoming IAEM-USA President Josh Morton, CEM. From L-R: Nicole Blankenship, CAE; Josh Morton; and Gretchen Birt.

The International Association of Emergency Managers (IAEM) is a non-profit educational organization dedicated to promoting the "Principles of Emergency Management" and representing those professionals whose goals are saving lives and protecting property and the environment during emergencies and disasters.
