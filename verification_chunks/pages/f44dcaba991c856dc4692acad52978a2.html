About
Know where your water comes from and how it is treated to help you take steps to avoid illness.

Causes of Contamination
Tap water can get contaminated by human or animal poop, chemicals, or other sources.
July 31, 2024
Prevention
Take steps at home and while traveling to avoid getting sick from drinking water.
May 21, 2024
Well Water Safety
Keep your well water safe with proper well construction and continued maintenance.
June 4, 2024

Featured

Waterborne Germs at Home
Avoid germs in water pipes and devices by taking steps like flushing faucets and cleaning devices.
Mar. 15, 2024
Communication Resources Toolkit
Download, use, and share fact sheets and social media about how to avoid getting sick from water.
May 27, 2025
Drinking Water Sources
Most U.S. tap water comes from reservoirs, lakes, rivers, or water under the ground.
Water Treatment
Water utilities remove harmful germs and chemicals from water to make it safe to drink.
Water Filters
Test your water and choose a filter that removes the germs or chemicals you are concerned about.
Safe Water When Traveling
If you are not sure if water is safe to drink, boil it or use another method to remove germs.
Recycled Water
Recycled (reused) tap water must meet safe drinking water standards.

Drinking Water
